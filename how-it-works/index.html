How it works

A minimal path from intake to provider review

No subscriptions, refill tracking, or messaging in this MVP — just the essentials.

  1. Step 1

    Complete your eligibility check

    Answer short questions about your health, location, and treatment interest. Colorado patients only.

  2. Step 2

    Submit your medical intake

    Create an account and complete a secure medical questionnaire. Save and continue later anytime.

  3. Step 3

    Provider review

    A Colorado-licensed provider reviews your intake and decides whether treatment may be appropriate. Prescribing is never guaranteed.
